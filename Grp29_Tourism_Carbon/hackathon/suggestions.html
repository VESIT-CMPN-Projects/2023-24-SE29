Steps to reduce personal Carbon Footprint

Direct Flights

Taking direct flights can reduce the number of stoppages and help in reducing the emissions.

Trains and Buses

Consider using trains or buses for shorter distances. As they produce fewer emissions than individual car travel.

Accomodations

Choose accommodations that have implemented sustainable practices, such as energy-efficient lighting and waste recycling programs.

Conserve Water and Energy

Practice water and energy conservation in hotels by reusing towels, turning off lights and electronics when not in use, and adjusting thermostat settings.

Practices to avoid

Over Packing

Carrying excess luggage increases the weight of the vehicle or aircraft, leading to higher fuel consumption. Pack efficiently and only bring what you need.

Single use plastics

Avoid using single-use plastics such as water bottles, straws, and utensils. Bring a reusable water bottle and shopping bag instead.

Wasteful Consumption

Be conscious of your consumption habits, such as excessive use of disposable products, and try to minimize wastage of water during your travels.

Renting Gas-Guzzling Vehicles

Avoid selecting larger vehicles, SUVs, or luxury cars, as they generally consume more fuel. Opt for smaller, fuel-efficient options.
